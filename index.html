Projects

Here are some of my completed and ongoing projects

HOPE


A multiplayer horror game where players must complete challenging objectives, such as unhinging a demon on an isolated farm. The game's AI is able to listen to players' actions and decisions, adapting in real-time: it can start hunting them down or strategically move away, adding a unique layer of tension and strategy to the game. Although it is only 90% complete, with the addition of final sounds and tweaks, it offers an immersive and chilling experience for fans of the genre.

SecretForest and Ferapet


I am currently part of a development team at a company dedicated to the creation of SecretForest and Ferapet. Secretforest, an MMORPG game. In my role, I am in charge of implementing and optimizing the combat system and managing the storage of information in the database, as well as overseeing the configuration and maintenance of the server instances. For Ferapet, I develop the session system, the creature capture system (feras), the Ferapedia, as well as the inventory and the world item drops and chests system. Due to a confidentiality agreement, I cannot disclose images or media from either project. Website link SecretForest

MMORPG


I am working on a personal project to develop an MMORPG that will allow players to register their accounts both from my portfolio and from within the game. In this game, players will be able to transform into animals, dominate them and capture them, either through combat or in a strategic manner. This project is an opportunity to combine all my frontend and backend skills, implementing a complete system that covers account creation, user management and gameplay experience. Through this development, I seek to demonstrate my technical and creative capabilities in creating an immersive and functional environment.
